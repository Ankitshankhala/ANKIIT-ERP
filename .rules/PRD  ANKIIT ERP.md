# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

**Product Name**: **ANKIIT ERP** – Custom Full Stack ERP SaaS Platform
**Date**: August 14, 2025

***

## 1. Overview

**ANKIIT ERP** is an integrated, full stack ERP SaaS platform delivering modular business management tools via a secure, scalable cloud service. It is designed for businesses of all sizes, offering customizable workflows, robust analytics, seamless integrations, and powerful multi-tenant SaaS capabilities.

***

## 2. Objectives

- Provide an all-in-one cloud ERP with modular features for finance, HR, CRM, inventory, manufacturing, and more.
- Enable rapid, secure onboarding for multiple organizations (multi-tenancy) with tenant-level customizability.
- Automate billing, updates, and support to maximize scalability and uptime.
- Deliver powerful data analytics, reporting, and compliance capabilities through **ANKIIT ERP**’s unified platform.

***

## 3. Target Users

- Small, medium, and large enterprises
- Industry verticals: manufacturing, retail, service providers, logistics, etc.
- System administrators, operations, finance, HR, IT, sales, and executive teams using **ANKIIT ERP** for daily business management

***

## 4. Key Features \& Modules

### Core ERP Modules in **ANKIIT ERP**

- **Finance \& Accounting**: General ledger; accounts payable/receivable; cash flow; expense and revenue tracking; payroll \& tax; reporting.
- **Sales \& Order Management**: Quotations, orders, invoices, pipeline; automated process flows; real-time tracking.
- **CRM**: Customer profiles; lead management; analytics; multi-channel communication; campaigns.
- **HR \& Payroll**: Personnel records; onboarding; attendance; leave and payroll; benefits admin; employee portal.
- **Inventory \& Warehouse**: Real-time stock; multi-location; batch/serial tracking; purchase orders.
- **Supply Chain \& Procurement**: Vendors; procurement flows; delivery and logistics; supplier performance.
- **Manufacturing \& Production**: BOM; production scheduling; shop floor management; resource allocation.
- **Project Management**: Gantt charts, budgets, timelines, collaboration.
- **Business Intelligence \& Reporting**: Custom and scheduled reports; dashboards; analytics; predictive insights.
- **Quality \& Compliance**: Audit trails; defect logging; regulatory documents.


### SaaS-Specific Features of **ANKIIT ERP**

- **Multi-Tenancy**: Logical/physical data separation; tenant-specific branding, settings, and workflows.
- **Subscription \& Billing**: Automated metered billing; invoicing; payment gateways; plan management.
- **Security \& Compliance**: Role-based access; audit logs; encryption; GDPR/HIPAA-ready.
- **Flexible Integrations**: RESTful APIs; connectors for CRM, ecommerce, accounting, and banking.
- **User Management**: SSO; hierarchical permissions; onboarding; self-registration.
- **Performance \& Monitoring**: Uptime checks; resource monitoring; system logs.
- **Automated Updates**: CI/CD pipelines; zero-downtime deployments; rollback on failure.
- **Mobile \& Web Access**: Responsive web UI; native/hybrid mobile app.
- **Service \& Support**: Ticketing; live chat; integrated knowledge base.


### Optional Modules (if prioritized in **ANKIIT ERP**)

- Asset Management
- R\&D Management
- Service Desk
- Document Management

## 5. Technical Requirements

- **Frontend**: React/Angular/Vue.js + TypeScript
- **Backend**: Node.js/.NET/Java/Python (Django preferred)
- **Database**: PostgreSQL/MySQL (relational), MongoDB (NoSQL)
- **Cloud Provider**: AWS, Azure, or GCP
- **Containerization \& CI/CD**: Docker, Kubernetes, GitHub Actions/Jenkins
- **Event/Message Queue**: RabbitMQ/Kafka
- **Search Engine**: Elasticsearch
- **APIs**: RESTful, OIDC/OAuth for SSO

***

## 6. Non-Functional Requirements for **ANKIIT ERP**

- SLA: 99.9% uptime
- Global scalability (region-based deployment)
- Data backup and disaster recovery
- Compliance (GDPR, role-based workflows)
- Localization (multi-language, currency/time zones)
- Accessibility WCAG 2.1

## 9. Risks \& Mitigations

- **Integration delays**: Use open API standards and phased rollout.
- **Compliance complexity**: Modularize compliance for different regions.
- **Scaling challenges**: Use microservices and auto-scaling on demand.
- **Security threats**: Implement continuous monitoring, advanced encryption, and strict access control.

- Legacy system migration tools (initially)
